# Hair Biolabs — Customer Service Policies

## Shipping Policy
- Orders processed and shipped within 24 business hours from confirmation.
- Delivery: 48 to 72 business hours depending on destination.
- Customers receive tracking information via email once orders are dispatched.
- Cash on delivery is available — customer must pay full amount to courier.
- Peak periods (Black Friday, Christmas, special promotions) may cause minor delays.
- Questions about shipments: contacto@hairbiolabs.com

## Refund Policy — 120-Day Money-Back Guarantee
- If no results are seen after 120 days of constant use, money is returned.
- Requirements to qualify:
  1. Take monthly photos of treated areas from the same angle with consistent lighting throughout the 120-day period (4 photos total).
  2. Apply the Hair Biolabs Serum 2 times daily (morning and night) on affected areas.
  3. Use the serum continuously for 120 days.
- To request a refund:
  1. Email info@hairbiolabs.com with subject line "Solicitud de Reembolso 120 Dias"
  2. Attach all 4 monthly photographs.
  3. Response within 7 business days maximum.
  4. Refund processed through original payment method once validated.
- IMPORTANT: Requests outside the 120-day period or without following the instructions will NOT be accepted.

## Subscription Policy
- Subscriptions offer recurring deliveries based on selected duration and frequency.
- Minimum commitment: 2 months (two full payments required before cancellation is permitted).
- Cancellation requires a minimum of 5 calendar days advance notice before next shipping date.
- Late cancellations cannot stop shipments already in logistics.
- Cancel via customer portal or by emailing contacto@hairbiolabs.com.
- Subscribers can pause, modify addresses, change payment methods, or adjust frequency through the customer portal or support email.
- All shipments are automatic per chosen frequency, with recurring charges to the original payment method.
- Subscriptions include an exclusive reduced price for committed subscribers.
